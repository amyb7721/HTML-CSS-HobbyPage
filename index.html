Obstacle Course Running: Spartan Races

Types of Races:

1.) Spartan Sprint

3 mile run with 20 obstacles.

2.) Spartan Super

8 mile run with 25 or more obstacles.

3.) Spartan Beast

13 mile run with 30 or more obstacles.

4.) Spartan Hurricane Heat 24 Hour Run

Hurricane Heat is a mainly team-based event designed to take your mental and physical capabilities to new limits.
